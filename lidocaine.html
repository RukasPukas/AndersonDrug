Indications:

Alternate to amiodarone in cardiac arrest from VT, VF, Stable wide-complex tachycardia (poly-or monomorphic) with normal baseline QT interval. Also used as a local anesthetic for various procedures, including intubation and IO infusion.

Protocols:

Adult

Tachycardia (with a Pulse) - Wide Complex;
Cardiac Arrest - (VFib / Pulseless V-Tach);
Intraosseous Access-Responsive to pain

Pediatric

Tachycardia (with a Pulse) - Wide Complex;
Cardiac Arrest - (Vfib / Pulseless V-Tach);
Intraosseous Access-Responsive to pain
