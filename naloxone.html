Anderson EMS Drug Dosages

Naloxone

Adult Dosage:

Adult
Poisoning and Overdose

IV or IM – 0.4-2.0 mg;

May repeat every 3-5 minutes until respiratory depression is reversed.

IN – 0.4 - 2 mg (1ml MAX) per nostril

Via atomizer, may repeat every 3-5 minutes until respiratory depression is reversed.

Note: Always ensure amounts are accurate prior to administration. Consult Anderson Protocols or Medical Control if uncertainty exists.
