Firefighter Rehabilitation

Initial Actions

1. Establish a Rehab Sector

2. Remove PPE from Firefighter

3. Establish Baseline Assessment
-Mental Status
-Skin Condition
-Baseline Vitals

Medical Assessment

1. Normal Mental Status

2. Heart Rate < 110

3. Systolic Blood Pressure < 150

4. Diastolic Blood Pressure < 100

5. Temperature < 100.6 F

No
Standard Rehab

1. Hydrate orally with 16 oz of fluids

2. Implement passive cooling measures with sensitivity to environment/shelter

3. Rest for 10 minutes

4. If medical assessment remains within normal limits as listed above, release from rehab after 10 minutes, if not refer to MEDICAL REHAB

Return to Staging
Medical Rehab

1. Consider move to Ambulance for further treatment

2. Implement active cooling (wet towels and ice)

3. Hydrate orally 32 oz. of fluids

4. Remain in Rehab for 15 to 20 minutes

5. Reassess medically every 5 minutes

Return to normal?
Yes
No
