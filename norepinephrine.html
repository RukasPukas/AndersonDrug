Norepinephrine

Adult Dosage:

Adult
CHF / Pulmonary Edema;
ROSC;
Sepsis;
Shock;
2-30 mcg/min IV/IO

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control. Always check dosages and concentrations prior to administration.

Pediatric Dosage:

Not indicated in pediatric populations.

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control. Always check dosages and concentrations prior to administration.

Indications:

Cardiogenic shock unresponsive to fluid resuscitation, significant hypotensive (Less than 70mmHg systolic) states, first-line vasopressor in septic shock; IV Pump available.
