Anderson EMS Drug Dosages

Albuterol

Adult Dosage:

Broncho Spasm: 5mg via Nebulizer, may repeat x2
Suspected Hyperkalemia: 10mg via Nebulizer, may repeat x2

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control.

Pediatric Dosage:

Patient ≤ 20kg: 2.5mg via Nebulizer, may repeat x2
Patient ≥ 20kg: 5mg via Nebulizer, may repeat x2

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control.

Indications:

Persistent bronchospasm, COPD exacerbation, Hyperkalemia

Protocols:

Adult: Allergic Reaction / Anaphylaxis; Bronchospasm / Asthma / COPD; Tracheostomy Tube-Respiratory Distress/Ventilator; Crush Injuries; Diabetic Emergencies-Hyperglycemia

Pediatric: Allergic Reaction / Anaphylaxis; Pediatric Respiratory Distress; Pediatric Respiratory Distress-Tracheostomy Tube
