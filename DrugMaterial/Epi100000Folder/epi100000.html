Anderson EMS Pocket Medic

Epinephrine 1:100,000
(Push Dose)

Adult Dosage:

Bradycardia; CHF / Pulmonary Edema; ROSC; Sepsis; Shock

Push Dose Epinephrine 1 mL (10 mcg) (1:100,000)

IV/IO every 2-5 minutes to maintain SBP of 90-100 mmHg or MAP > 65 mmHg

Method:

(Mix 1 mL of Epinephrine 1:10,000 with 9 mL of Normal Saline in a 10 mL syringe resulting in a concentration of 10 mcg/ml)

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control. Always check dosages and concentrations prior to administration.

Pediatric Dosage:

Not indicated for pediatric patiens without consulting medical control.

Indications:

Bradycardia, CHF / Pulmonary Edema, ROSC with hypotension/bradycardia, Sepsis, Shock
