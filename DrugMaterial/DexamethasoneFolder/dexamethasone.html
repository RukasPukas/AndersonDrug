Anderson EMS Pocket Medic

Dexamethasone

Adult Dosage:

Allergic Reaction/Anaphylaxis; Bronchospasm/Asthma/COPD;

10mg IV/IO


Altitude Illness-AMS/HACE

8mg IM, IV, or PO (administered adjunctively with descent). Followed by 4mg every 6 hours.

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control. Always check dosages and concentrations prior to administration.

Pediatric Dosage:

Not indicated in pediatrics.

Indications:

Used in management of croup and bronchospasm, as well as the management of patients suffering from high altitude cerebral edema.

Protocols:

Allergic Reaction/Anaphylaxis;
Bronchospasm/Asthma/COPD;
