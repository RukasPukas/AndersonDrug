Anderson EMS Pocket Medic

Metoprolol

Adult Dosage:

Metoprolol: 5 mg IV over 1-2 min. Repeat as needed every 5 min if SBP > 100mmHg (Max 3 doses).

Pediatric Dosage:

Metoprolol is not indicated in pediatric patients per Anderson protocols at this time.

Indications:

For management of narrow complex tachycardias

Protocols:

Adult
Tachycardia (Symptomatic with a pulse);
