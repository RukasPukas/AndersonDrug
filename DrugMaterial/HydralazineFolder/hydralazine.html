Hydralazine

Adult Dosage:

Eclampsia/ Pre-Eclampsia

5 mg IV

May repeat 10 mg after 20 min for persistent hypertension if MAP has not been reduced by 20-25% after the initial dose.


Pediatric Dosage:

Not Indicated In Pediatric Patients.

Indications:

Severe hypertension with pre-eclampsia symptoms.
