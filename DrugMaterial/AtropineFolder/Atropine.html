Atropine Sulfate

Adult Dosage:

Bradycardia: 1.0 mg IV/IO every 3-5 minutes, as long as symptomatic bradycardia persists, to a max total dose of 3mg

Acetylcholinesterase Inhibitors: 2mg IV or IM; repeat at 2-4mg IV every 3-5 minutes until symptoms of SLUDGE subside, most importantly secretions

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control. Always check dosages and concentrations prior to administration.
