Anderson EMS Pocket Medic

TXA

Adult Dosage:

Adult

1 gram over 10 minutes

Instructions:

Mix 1 gram/10mL vial in 100mL NS and administer over 10 minutes IV at a wide open rate.

If unable to be given IV/IO, TXA 1 gram/10mL may given deep IM utilizing two injection sites by splitting the standard dose into two (500mg/5mL) doses.

NOTE:

*According to the manufacturer, TXA should be given via a dedicated line.*

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control. Always check dosages and concentrations prior to administration.

Pediatric Dosage:

Not indicated in pediatric populations.

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control. Always check dosages and concentrations prior to administration.

Indications:

Blunt or penetrating trauma less than 3 hours from onset with hemodynamic compromise, bleeding

Contraindications:

Hypersensitivity; MOI greater than 3 hours; history of PE, DVT, or other thromboembolic disorder
