Anderson EMS Pocket Medic

Nitroglycerin

Adult Dosage:

Adult
Chest Pain / Acute Coronary Syndrome / STEMI;
CHF / Pulmonary Edema;

Sublingual:

0.4 mg SL;

May repeat every 3-5 minutes to maximum of 3 doses as long as chest pain persists and SBP greater than 90 mmHg or MAP greater than 70 mmHg.

Topical (paste):

1 inch

SBP must be greater than 100 mmHg (remove if SBP becomes lower less than 100 mmHg).

Pediatric Dosage:

Not indicated in pediatric populations.

If uncertainty arises, consult Anderson Protocols or contact Medical Control. Always check dosages and concentrations prior to administration.

Indications:

Acute angina pectoris, ischemic chest pain, hypertension, heart failure, pulmonary edema.
