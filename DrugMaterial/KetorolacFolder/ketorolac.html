Anderson EMS Pocket Medic

Ketorolac

Adult Dosage:

Pain Management

30 mg IM or 15 mg IV (no repeat dose)


Pediatric Dosage:

Not Indicated In Pediatric Patients.

Indications:

Short-term management of moderate to severe pain.

Contraindications:

Allergy to aspirin, ketorolac, or other NSAIDS; Pregnant females; Patients with history of asthma, bleeding disorders (especially GI related, such as peptic ulcer disease), renal failure.

Protocols:

Adult
Pain Management
