Pediatric Dosage:

Not indicated for pediatric patients.

Indications:

Anesthesia for orotracheal intubation; procedural sedation (e.g. synchronized cardioversion).

Contraindications:

Use with caution in patients with sepsis, hepatic disease and renal impairment.

Protocols:

Assisted (Medication) Intubation;
Tachycardia (synchronized cardioversion);
