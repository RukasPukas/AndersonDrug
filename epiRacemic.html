Racemic Epinephrine

Adult Dosage:

Not indicated in adult populations.

Pediatric Dosage:

0.5 mL of 2.25% solution diluted in 3 mL NS nebulized.

-OR-

If racemic epinephrine is not available for treating an upper airway disease process (stridor), nebulize 3.0 mg ((x3) Epinephrine 1:1000 mixed with 3mL of Normal Saline).

Indications:

Bronchial asthma, prevention of bronchospasm, croup, laryngeal edema.
